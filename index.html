How to Play

The rules are simple. You play against a computer till one of you gets 20 points or more. You both roll the dice by clicking the dice block. When you land on a blue space, you get 3 points, but if you land on a red space, you lose 3 points. A green space can do one of three things: (1) Give you ten points (2) Take away 10 points (don't worry, you can never go below zero), or spaces places with the other player.
