Welcome to Mario Party Basic! A simplified version of the iconic video game franchise. The rules are simple: You'll hit the dice when it's your turn. When you land a blue space, you get 3 coins! When you land on a red space, you lose 3 coins. The first person to get 20 coins wins! Also you'll have the ability to go on a different branch on you traverse the board.
